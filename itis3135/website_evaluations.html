Website Evaluations

Volvo Cars

  • Contrast: Strong use of white space against high-resolution imagery and bold typography makes the content easy to read.
  • Repetition: Consistent use of the Volvo Broad font and minimalist button styles creates a unified brand experience.
  • Alignment: Elements are strictly aligned to a grid, providing a clean, organized, and professional layout.
  • Proximity: Related information (like car specs and pricing) is grouped tightly, reducing cognitive load for the user.

Amazon

  • Contrast: The page is often cluttered with too many competing colors, banners, and buttons, making it hard to find a focal point.
  • Repetition: While the logo is consistent, the vast variety of card styles and ad formats feels disjointed and repetitive in a chaotic way.
  • Alignment: Information is packed into every corner, leading to a "wall of text" feel where alignment is often sacrificed for sheer volume.
  • Proximity: Sponsored items are often mixed closely with organic results, confusing the relationship between different types of content.
